THE VERANDAH

Welcome !


We have been serving your international favourites since 2012 from our restaurant in Kuala Lumpur. Situated in The Life Centre, Jalan Sultan Ismail, our dedicated team has been creating dishes that are at the forefront of Kuala Lumpur's foodie's and Instagrammer's minds. We warmly welcome you to come and savour your favourite dishes from our dedicated chefs. Come for lunch, dinner or just coffee and desserts!
